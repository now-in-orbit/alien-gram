Dib Membrane

Persona

Technology

  • Devices: Dell XPS Desktop Computer running Ubuntu 20.04 and mainly uses Google Chrome. Dib also uses a Google Pixel 4xl running Android 11
  • Proficiency: Dib is very technically proficient. He also spends a lot of his time researching new technologies to continue growing his knowledge.
  • Passion for technology: Dib is extremely passionate about technology. If he had to choose between his desktop and his mom he would undeniably choose his desktop

Attitudes and Needs

  • Dib is mainly using this site because he wants to gain and spread information on alien sightings.
  • Dib needs a site that he is able to see other Alien Enthusiasts information and spread his own information to his colleagues
  • Dib has chosen to use AlienGram due to the lack of platforms for his use and the simplicity of AlienGram

Frustrations

  • Dib sometimes finds himself frustrated when he is not able to find a reliable site to spread and gather information on aliens
  • Dib is also frustrated when people outright post fake or misleading information
  • Dib is also frustrated when he is unable to weigh in on the validity of a potential sighting

Role

  • Power User

User Story

As a power user I want to gather and spread alien information.

Use Case

Dib has recently taken 4 photographs of what he beleives to be a UFO. He would like to share these pictures wth other alien enthusiasts. Dib has recently found AlienGram and is now going to upload his images and provide a description of his encounter.

  • PreCondition: Dib accesses AlienGram website
  • PostCondition: Dib is now a registered user and creates his first post

Interaction Flow

  • Dib clicks on "Sign Up" option
  • AlienGram displays form with "Email", "Username", "Password", and "Confirm Password"options
  • Dib fills in required information
  • AlienGram prompts Dib to prove he is not a robot using a reCaptcha
  • Dib completes reCaptcha
  • AlienGram confirms he is at least not a robot...Redirects to home page
  • Dib clicks on "Post!" button in the top right of his screen
  • AlienGram displays opens a form with various fields(Date/Time, City/State, Duration and Description) for him to fill out
  • Dib fills out the required information and looks for a way to attach his images. He clicks on the paperclip icon in bottom of form
  • AlienGram displays an area for user to attach images(jpeg, png, etc)
  • Dib uploads images of his interaction
  • AlienGram displays a "Are you sure?" box with the options "Yes" & "No"
  • Dib clicks on "Yes" in order to confirm his post
  • AlienGram posts Dib's post to the feed of other reports
