Persona Zim

Zim Invid

Profession

Zim works at Walmart as merchandise and stocking associate in the mornings. He has a lot of time in his day since he works so early in the morning. He is going to school for business but is undecided on the type of business to start. He just knows he wants to be his own boss.

Technology:

  • Acer Spin 3 14 inch Touchscreen 2-in-1 Laptop, AMD Ryzen 3 3250u, 4GB Ram, 128gb running latest version of Windows OS. His browser of choice is mozilla firefox.
  • iPhone 8 256GB 4.7-inch widescreen iOS 13
  • Proficiency: Zim is not the most tech savvy person but he has a pretty broad understanding of social media apps. He has a computer that uses as a part-time student at a community college and that's the only time he ever really uses it. He knows the basics of Microsoft office. He uses his phone predominantly to record videos and take photos for social media to entertain people on Instagram and Tik Tok.
  • Zim's lack of knowledge when it comes to computers has lead to frustrations with technology and a love/hate relationship with it.

Attitudes & Needs:

  • Zim needs a new social media platform as new entertainment.
  • Zim recently watched the hit movie Men in Black and it peaked his interest in the possibility of aliens.

Frustrations

  • His posts keep getting reported on Instagram and Tik Tok.
  • Other platforms that he has found did not keep geographical locations of alien sightings.
  • Other alien specific sites don't allow him to make posts.
  • Other platforms only show sightings from decades ago.

Role

  • Casual user

User Story

As a casual user, I want to explore posts of alien sightings nearby.

Use Case

Zim accesses Aliengram map page and sees a map with various dots and becomes curious as to what they are.

  • Pre-condition: Zim stumbles upon Aliengram map page.
  • Post-condition: Zim learns that each dot on the map serves as a geographical representation of an extraterrestrial sighting.

Interaction FLow

  • Zim opens the map page.
  • Aliengram displays map.
  • Zim hovers over map.
  • Map displays integer of number of alien sightings per state.
  • Zim clicks a dot on the map.
  • A pop-up of the alien sighting appears
  • Zim reads the pop-up.
  • He clicks on the close button to exit the pop-up.
  • Pop-up closes and returns to display of map.
